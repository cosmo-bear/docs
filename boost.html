Boosts Guide

In Cosmo Bear, boosts help your bear travel farther and faster. There are three types of boosts: Fuel Capacity, Fuel Recharge Speed, and Speed. Each boost has multiple levels that increase its effectiveness.

Fuel Capacity Boost

The Fuel Capacity Boost increases the amount of fuel your bear can hold, allowing you to tap for longer before running out of fuel. Here are the levels:

  • Level 1: 1,000 max capacity, Price: 0
  • Level 2: 1,500 max capacity, Price: 100
  • Level 3: 2,000 max capacity, Price: 200
  • Level 4: 2,500 max capacity, Price: 400
  • Level 5: 3,000 max capacity, Price: 800
  • Level 6: 3,500 max capacity, Price: 1,600

Fuel Recharge Speed Boost

The Fuel Recharge Speed Boost increases the rate at which your bear's fuel recharges, measured in km per second. Here are the levels:

  • Level 1: 1 per second, Price: 0
  • Level 2: 2 per second, Price: 100
  • Level 3: 3 per second, Price: 200
  • Level 4: 4 per second, Price: 400

Speed Boost

The Speed Boost increases the distance your bear travels per tap. Here are the levels:

  • Level 1: 1 km/tap, Price: 0
  • Level 2: 2 km/tap, Price: 100
  • Level 3: 3 km/tap, Price: 200
  • Level 4: 4 km/tap, Price: 400
  • Level 5: 5 km/tap, Price: 800
  • Level 6: 6 km/tap, Price: 1,600
