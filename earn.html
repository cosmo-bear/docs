Earn Guide

In Cosmo Bear, you can earn coins by tapping and traveling through space. Here's how you can maximize your earnings

Basic Earning

Every tap helps Barry travel 1 KM, earning you 1 coin. Initially, each tap travels 1 KM, but you can increase the distance traveled per tap by purchasing boosts. More distance per tap means more coins earned per tap!

Tip: Use boosts to increase your KM per tap and maximize your coin earnings!

Exploring Galaxies

When you complete traveling through a galaxy, you will earn a significant amount of coin rewards for your exploration. Keep exploring new galaxies to earn more coins!

Completing Challenges

In the Earn section or via your radar, you will find various challenges that offer different coin rewards. These challenges are designed to help Barry reach his goals faster. Make sure to complete them all to maximize your earnings!

Inviting Friends

Invite your friends to join Cosmo Bear and earn more rewards. For every friend who joins, you and your friend will receive coin bonuses. Plus, you'll earn a percentage of the rewards your friends collect, making it a win-win situation!

Tip: The more friends you invite, the more coins you can earn through their activities!

Space Treasures

Space is full of treasures, and Barry is collecting them. Each tap earns Barry more coins, and using boosts can increase his collection speed. Complete tasks and quests to earn extra coins. These challenges are simple and will help Barry reach his goals.

End-of-Season Airdrop

Once the season ends, you will receive Barry's coins through an Airdrop. Keep tapping and earning to make the most of this end-of-season reward!

Start tapping, exploring, completing challenges, and inviting friends to maximize your coin earnings in Cosmo Bear!
