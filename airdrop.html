Cosmo Bear's Airdrop of $CBX Tokens

Cosmo Bear's airdrop of $CBX Tokens is scheduled for December 24, 2024. These tokens will be distributed on the Coreum Layer-1 Blockchain. To participate in the airdrop and receive your tokens, it is crucial to ensure that your wallet is activated and compatible with the Coreum network. This ensures a seamless and secure transaction, allowing you to benefit from the airdrop without any issues.

By integrating with Coreum, Cosmo Bear ensures that the $CBX Token airdrop is executed on a reliable and sophisticated blockchain platform, offering participants the benefits of a secure, scalable, and highly interoperable network.
