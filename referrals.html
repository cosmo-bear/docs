Referrals Guide

Earn more rewards by inviting your friends on your space journey!

  • When you refer a friend, both you and your friend receive 50,000 coins.
  • If your friend invites another person, you receive an additional 15,000 coins, and both the new person and your friend receive 50,000 coins.
  • You earn 50% of all rewards earned by your direct referrals.
  • You earn 25% of all rewards earned by your referrals' referrals.

Example: You invite Alice, and she joins Cosmo Bear. Both you and Alice get 50,000 coins. Alice then invites Bob. Alice gets 50,000 coins, Bob gets 50,000 coins, and you get 15,000 coins. Additionally, you will earn 50% of all rewards Alice earns and 25% of all rewards Bob earns.

Cybertruck

Raffle Details:

  • 1 raffle ticket for every 10 referrals.
  • Higher chances of winning with more tickets.
